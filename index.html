Maurice Bailleu

I am a senior database researcher at Huawei R&D UK, based in Edinburgh. In addition to focusing on database systems, I am also interested in designing high-end distributed trusted storage systems.

Before joining Huawei, I got my PhD from the Univerity of Edinburgh, supervised by Prof. Pramod Bhatotia.

Interests: Distributed and Dependable Systems, Secure Storage, File Systems, Intel SGX, Operating Systems, Parallel Architectures, Memory System.
